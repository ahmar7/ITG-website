About Us

BlockSkill is a Cloud-based Skilling Management System (SMS) that enables companies, organizations, and educators to create, implement & managed rich, interactive, and intuitive Corporate Open Online Courses (COOC) by reducing training costs and helping the future workforce to develop the right skills.


For Microsoft clients, BlockSkill is also a customer (CxP) and learner experience (LxP) portal where all their skilling information is gathered and organized for their skilling and certification plans related to Microsoft’s initiatives and offerings.
